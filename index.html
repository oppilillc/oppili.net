Highlighted Case Story:

Johnson & Johnson / Janssen Mobile App

Overview and Business Need

Janssen is currently challenged with significant time delays, inefficiencies, and extra costs when reconciling expenses into Concur associated with business and sales meetings and special events. There is an ongoing risk with inaccurate inputs of data such as names and addresses, adding to a flawed database. Janssen needed a digital mobile App that will ensure speed, accuracy and significant cost savings.

Project Description and Goals

The new App named “CAPP” will enable sales managers to focus on building relationships, elevating partnerships and increasing sales opportunities. It will streamline the process before the event date, enable the optimal management during the day of, and empower users to complete the event by reconciling all expenses into Concur successfully and accurately. The mobile App is very easy to use, fast in completing tasks, and informative with real time data and transparency into all aspects of their event management, therefore empowering users to have more successful outcomes.
